Emergency Escalation

How It Works

Simple. Fast. Reliable.

The patient journey from registration to completion — in five clear steps.

01

Create an Account

Register once with your name and email. Receive a unique Patient ID for all future visits.

02

Book an Appointment

Select your visit type (Regular, Emergency, or Follow-Up), choose a preferred doctor or auto-assign, and enter the queue instantly.

03

Track Your Position

Your dashboard shows your live queue number and a decreasing estimated wait time — updated automatically.

04

Consult Your Doctor

Your assigned doctor reviews your history and uploaded files before the session, then marks you complete or schedules follow-up care.

05

Admin Oversight

Admins monitor the full queue, reassign doctors, override priorities, and manage staff — all from one control panel.

Roles

Three roles. One system.

A tailored experience built for everyone in your clinic.

Administrator

Add patients, assign doctors, escalate emergencies, delete staff, and oversee the entire clinic queue from a central control panel.

Doctor

View your assigned patient queue, access full medical history and files, mark sessions complete, and reassign when required.

Patient

Book appointments, track your queue position live, update your medical profile, and upload documents — from any device.
